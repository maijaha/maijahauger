I enjoy working with a social approach to design.

As a designer I like to learn new things, both academic and situated exploration driven by curiosity. Design is meaningful to me when it enhances reflexivity by creating space for action and reflection. I have a wide range of experience with material (a bit of a generalist), and especially like visual design and weird crafting of methods and concepts.
